Our process

Our approach is to bring simplicity in each and every work flow. From the moment you contact us, we work tirelessly to make the hiring process as straightforward for you as we possibly can. And that's why we've adopted this simple step by step approach.
  • Submit a detailed description and information regarding the level of experience, qualifications and skills that you're looking for in a prospective employee.
  • Our recruitment consultants work hard to find a pool of candidates from our in-house team or from outside resources that we believe have the required skills to add value to your business.
  • After we've pre-screened all prospective candidates, we will submit a list of only the most suitable candidates.
  • From the list of possible candidates, you choose the candidates to be interviewed.
  • An Account manager or a Project manager would be assigned for you as well. The account manager arranges the kick off meeting for introduction of both the teams.
  • You would start allocating work to the team on our PM tool and get to see daily reports and time-sheets.
  • Once the interview process is complete, you select the candidate that you would like to employ. If you don't feel that any of the candidates you interviewed quite fitted the bill, just let us know, and we will happily comeback to you with another list of highly capable options.
